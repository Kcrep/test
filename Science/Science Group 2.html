Wind Turbines

Wind turbines generate power by rotating propeller-like blades around a central rotor, which drives a generator to produce electricity. This process harnesses the kinetic energy of the wind rather than using electricity to create wind.

Solar Panels

Solar panels utilize photovoltaic cells to convert sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity, which can be used for various devices or stored in batteries. This conversion process involves exciting electrons, allowing them to flow through a circuit and generate electricity.

Rain Harvesting System

Rainwater harvesting involves collecting, redirecting, and storing rainwater from rooftops for various uses, such as toilet flushing, filling ornamental ponds, providing makeup water for cooling towers, landscape irrigation, and washing applications.

Airplane

An airplane is a type of aircraft equipped with wings and one or more engines, enabling it to travel through the air. While the term "aircraft" can refer to various flying vehicles, including helicopters, airplanes specifically denote fixed-wing vehicles.

Car

A car is a wheeled motor vehicle primarily designed for road travel, typically accommodating one to eight passengers. It is mainly used for transporting people rather than goods.

Train

A train consists of a series of connected cars that transport passengers or cargo along a railroad track, providing an efficient means of mass transit over land.
